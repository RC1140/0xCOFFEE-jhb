0xC0FFEE Cape Town is an off-shoot from ZaCon (a South African Hacker Conference), aiming to connect the hacker community in Cape Town. Our meetups tend to be more social in nature, though we do try and ensure a relevant topic of discussion to encourage learning. Attendance is free and open to all - regardless of skill level or experience.
